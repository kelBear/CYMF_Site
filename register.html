Registration Deadline for the 2013 Festival is now extended to April 26, 2013. Currently, potential applicants have two ways to register for the upcoming CYMF taking place in May, 2013:


1. Online Registration

2. Mail-In Registration

We encourage applicants to use our Online Registration for a quick and efficient registration process. If you would like to fill out a form by hand, please mail the completed registration form to:

CYMF

7 Birdsong Street

Richmond Hill, Ontario

L4E 5C8


Confirmation emails will be sent out starting from April 29, 2013, if you do not receive your confirmation information by May 08, 2013, please contact info@celebrationyouthmusicfestival.com.

*Please note that only one form per applicant per instrument. Each applicant is entitled to submit up to three entries per instrument. Exemptions include Class 37 (Open Class). Please consult to the 2013 Syllabus for more information.
