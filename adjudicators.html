Dennis Beck





Dennis Beck (BMus, BEd) began his musical teaching career in Edmonton, Alberta, where he taught in both junior and senior high schools. Performing as a professional clarinetist, most notably with the Edmonton Wind Sinfonia, he also taught and conducted ensembles at the Alberta College Conservatory. During his twelve years in Alberta, he obtained a graduate diploma in conducting from the University of Calgary. Since returning to Ontario in 1990, he has taught at Unionville High School in York Region for the Arts York Music Program. He is also a sessional lecturer for the Ontario Institute for Studies in Education at the University of Toronto. In addition, he has also been an adjudicator at music festivals, speaks at band clinic sessions, and is a busy guest conductor. In 2000, Dennis helped establish the Ontario Band Association. Dennis Beck has made many important contributions to the education of music and has left lasting impressions to the musical community. After his retirement in 2011, he is currently an avid photographer and traveler, having visited almost 30 countries around the world. Dennis Beck is honored to be adjudicating at the first annual Celebration Youth Music Festival, and hopes to inspire young musicians to realize the power of music.

Jeff Wrigglesworth

Jeff Wrigglesworth (BMus, BEd) is a proud graduate of Unionville High School. He received his Honours Bachelor of Music Education from the University of Western Ontario and his Bachelor of Education from Nipissing University. He has been teaching in both elementary and secondary schools in York Region since 1995 and was thrilled to return to UHS as Head of Music in 2005. Jeff was a founding director of the Ontario Band Association as the Director of the Ontario Provincial Honour Band and continues to be a contributing member of that organization. He has been a clinician with the Ontario Music Educators' Association and was a recipient of an Ontario Band Association Concert Band Festival conducting award in 2011 and 2012. He is currently the chair of the York Region Music Heads Association, and is a recipient of a Tri-M Honours Music Society's Honourary Lifetime Membership. Whenever possible, Jeff continues to work as a trumpet player and clinician around the GTA. He lives in Markham with his wife Michelyn and extremely energetic and musical kids, Tricia and Warren.
