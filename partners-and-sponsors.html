Would you like to sponsor the 3rd annual Celebration Youth Music Festival? Read the rest of the page to find out how you can help make our event a success!

What?

Celebration Youth Music Festival is a non-profit children’s music festival that is organized by youth for youth—the first of its kind! It is designed to offer any music student studying piano, band, vocal or strings the opportunity to perform and enlighten their musical journey.

Where?

The event will be held at Unionville High School

When?

May 2014 (Exact date TBA)

Who?

200+ performing and supportive attendees

Why?

To promote music education and performance in youth.

How?

With your help! We are depending on our sponsors to make this non-profit event possible.

By supporting CYMF, you are supporting an event that stands apart from all other music festivals in the region. So how are we different?

  1. We deviate from the status quo.

    • CYMF challenge the restrictions other music festivals force upon their performers. We do not believe in putting limits on grade level or repertoire,. When performing, participants should be granted a sense of freedom and comfort.

  2. We are solely non-profit.

    • CYMF provides a fee-free environment for all our participants, thus opening doors to anyone who wishes to share their musical talent.

  3. We encourage youth to make a memorable contribution to the community.

    • As a committee member or participant, youth acquire a rewarding, unique experience by being involved in this public event.

  4. Lastly, we are the FIRST non-profit music festival for children in York Region. We are truly one of a kind!

What can you do to help?

  • Provide cash sponsorship

  • Provide product or vouchers for festival attendees

Logo Placement

  • Website

  • Flyers/Posters

  • Program brochures

  • Signage at event

Product Placement

  • We will place product or vouchers in attendee gift bags

Thank you for your time. We look forward to working with you to represent your brand at our event. Not only will sponsoring this event allow you to reach out to the community, you get to promote the importance of music in society as well. Every donation will be put into providing better musical instruments and equipment for our participating musicians, more accommodating venues, and greater events to help build youth's passion in music and the arts. Your donations will also benefit the community around us and help us spread our love of the arts to everyone.

For more information on how you can make a difference in a young musician's life, please contact Belinda Ding at belinda.ding@celebrationyouthmusicfestival.com or at (647) 896 9138.
