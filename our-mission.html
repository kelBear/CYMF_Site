At CYMF, we hope to....


Promote Musical Education and Performance...


CYMF is designed to provide children and youths with the opportunity to showcase their talents, develop good performance abilities and etiquettes, as well as receive professional and constructive commentaries from numerous musicians in all disciplines of music. This festival is meant to prepare today's young musician with outstanding knowledge of performance etiquettes as well as to educate youths and children about the importance of performing. At the same time, we hope that this festival will allow young musicians to comprehend and appreciate the pleasure and enjoyment of music performances.

Raise Money for Charities and Promote the Importance of Music in Society...

CYMF hopes to fundraise for charities that support and advocate the importance of music. Music should not be classified as an activity exclusive to those who can afford it. Music is for everyone. We hope to assist the movement of providing children the opportunity to explore music regardless of their financial, social or physical state. To conclude our festival, we will host a benefit concert featuring successful participants and guest artists. Through generous contributions, we hope to provide a charity with funds to support their benevolent cause. Let's all make a difference!
