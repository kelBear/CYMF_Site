Home > Past Events > Festival 2012

The 2012 festival is currently set to take place between May 31 - June 02, 2012.


Registration for the 2012 festival is now closed. All applicants wishing to participate in the festival must register.


Prizes and Awards

  • Everyone is a winner!

  • Certificates and special recognition will be awarded to outstanding participants in each individual class based on standards of Gold, Silver, or Bronze:


  • Mark of 85 and above: Gold

  • Mark of 80-84: Silver

  • Mark of 75-79: Bronze


  • All participants are invited to attend the gala concert, where additional special awards and certificates will be given to selected participants.

The festival will take place at Unionville High School.
